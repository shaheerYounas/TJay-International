Our inclusive service and fixed price guarantee

JAY GROUP PLC can build your house on a turn-key basis. This means we construct the complete outer structure for you, including the roof, roof drainage, external doors and windows.

We also do all the painting and decorating work, the floors are laid according to your choice of tile or carpet, and the bathrooms are equipped with sanitaryware and fittings. This option also includes complete electrical and plumbing installations, an Air Source Heat Pump with hot water cylinder and underfloor heating, plus a mechanical ventilation with heat recovery system. For a reasonable surcharge, you are able to upgrade your home with a Ground Source Heat Pump.

Energy efficiency

JAY GROUP PLC truly cares about the natural environment. Which is why we work so hard to make JAY GROUP PLC houses as energy efficient as possible.

JAY GROUP PLC houses walls have a coefficient of U=0,12 W/(m²K) and a thermal insulation of 30 cms. This thick insulation 'wraps' the house and protects it against both cold weather and overheating.

JAY GROUP PLC houses are almost 100% airtight. We achieve this by using modern construction technology to ensure that heat is kept inside the house.

Every JAY GROUP PLC house also features mechanical ventilation with a heat recovery system. This is extremely important in energy-efficient houses, as the continuous exchange of air provides a comfortable and pleasant atmosphere in the house at all times, and helps to reduce heating bills.

JAY GROUP PLC houses have a B Energy Efficiency Rating which is a measure of its overall efficiency. The higher the rating, the more energy efficient the house is, and the lower the fuel bills are likely to be. In buying an environmentally-friendly JAY GROUP PLC house, you can then benefit from much lower heating costs.

Renewable energy systems

JAY GROUP PLC is committed to providing renewable energy systems and you can benefit from a range of solar photovoltaic (PV) energy systems, heat pumps and battery storage.

Normally roof-top mounted, solar panels convert light directly into electricity which is used for heating and cooling. Battery systems are also available which store excess electricity in a battery for re-use. Solar PV panels can increase your EPC to an A rating and can also be used to heat water and charge electric cars.

PV panels convert the sun’s rays into electricity, even on cloudy days. A typical 3.5kWp system can generate around 3,000 kilowatt hours of electricity a year which is equal to about 75% of a typical household’s electricity needs.

All standard JAY GROUP PLC houses include an Air Source Heat Pump (ASHP) which absorbs heat from the air and transfers it inside to heat your home and provide all your hot water. Underfloor heating provides an even heat distribution throughout the house and can be zone or room controlled.

Here are some other benefits:

Save money

Your energy bills will drop, and you can also receive bonus payments for any surplus electricity that is fed back to the grid.

Low maintenance

Solar panels only need cleaned a couple of times a year and, with no moving parts, there is little wear and tear.

Clean Energy 

The initial cost of installing solar panels easily pays for itself in a few years, and it will increase the value of your house.
